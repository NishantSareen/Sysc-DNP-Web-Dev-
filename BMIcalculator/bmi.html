Total body fat is correlated to the risk if certain diseases which can be potentially fatal. BMI is valid for both men and women. However, it should only be used as a guidance as it has some limitations. It may overestimate the body fat in muscular persons and underestimate the body fat in person who have lost muscle mass.

More information can be found at this BMI Calculator website. This website is based on the formula obtained from that site.
